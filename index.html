aims & objectives

one

• To stimulate, nurture and adopt feelings of fraternity and Kinsmanship and to strengthen social bond in general and through cooperative efforts in particular.

two

• To encourage spirit of mutual help and goodwill among the Abakolwe Bamale community in general and the society members in particular for the aim of promoting harmony.

three

• To promote activities that are conducive to social, recreational, cultural, moral intellectual, economic development and other objectives that are of welfare in nature among the Abakolwe Bamale community in general and society members in particular

four

• Undertake research, document, publish and share any suitable literature to promote cause and objectives of the society.

five

• To cooperate and partner with societies and entities with similar objectives and functioning.

six

• To undertake all such lawful acts, deeds and endeavors including cultural events, charitable activities, educational events as are incidental and conducive to the overall wellbeing of the Abakolwe Bamale community in general and society members in particular

seven

• To mobilize resources and establish entities such as companies, partnerships, cooperatives for the purpose investment and economic wellbeing of the community in general and the society in particular
